How to Use

  1. Open Speak My Text and set your Resemble voice UUID (if not set already).
  2. Pick an emotion, adjust speed and pitch as needed.
  3. Try Ask → Answer to record a question; it will be answered and spoken back in your voice.
  4. Use Parrot to quickly validate audio clarity and loudness consistency.
